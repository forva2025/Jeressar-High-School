O-Level Admission

Entry Requirements

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) certificate
  • Minimum aggregate of 28 points
  • Birth certificate and passport photos
  • Recommendation letter from primary school

Age Requirements

Students should be between 12-14 years old at the time of admission.

Application Timeline

Applications open in January and close in March for the academic year starting in February.

A-Level Admission

Entry Requirements

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) or equivalent
  • Minimum of 5 credits including English and Mathematics
  • Relevant subject credits for chosen combination
  • Academic transcripts and conduct report

Subject Requirements

Science Combinations: Credits in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology

Arts Combinations: Credits in English, History, Geography, Economics

Application Timeline

Applications open in March and close in May for the academic year starting in June.
