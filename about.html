About Us

Discover our story, mission, and the dedicated team behind Jeressar High School's commitment to educational excellence.

Our History

Founded in 1999 by the Jeressar Educational Trust, our school began as a modest institution with a vision to provide quality education to the community of Soroti and beyond. What started with just 50 students and 5 teachers has grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Over the past 25 years, we have consistently adapted to the changing educational landscape while maintaining our commitment to excellence. Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, innovation, and a dedication to nurturing the holistic development of every student who walks through our doors.

Today, Jeressar High School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, serving over 1,200 students with a team of 50 dedicated educators. Our alumni have gone on to excel in universities worldwide and contribute meaningfully to society across various professions.

Mission, Vision & Values

The foundation of our educational philosophy and the guiding principles that shape every aspect of our institution.

Our Mission

To provide holistic, quality education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and innovative thinking, preparing students to become responsible global citizens and leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Uganda, recognized for producing well-rounded individuals who excel academically, demonstrate strong character, and drive positive change in their communities and beyond.

Core Values

  • Excellence in all endeavors
  • Integrity and moral uprightness
  • Respect and empathy
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Service to community
