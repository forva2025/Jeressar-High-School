Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our school.

What are the admission requirements?

For O-Level, students need a PLE certificate with a minimum aggregate of 28 points. For A-Level, students need UCE with at least 5 credits including English and Mathematics. Specific subject requirements apply for different combinations.

What is the school fee structure?

O-Level boarding fees are UGX 1,530,000 per year, while A-Level boarding fees are UGX 1,800,000 per year. Day student fees are lower. We offer flexible payment plans and scholarships for deserving students.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of activities including science club, debate society, music & drama, various sports (football, basketball, netball, athletics), entrepreneurship club, and community service programs.

What are the school hours?

Academic activities run from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM, with supervised self-study from 2:40 PM to 4:30 PM. Boarding students have additional study hours in the evening. Weekend activities are available for boarders.

Do you offer transportation services?

Yes, we provide school bus services for day students from various locations in Soroti and surrounding areas. Bus routes and fees are determined based on distance from the school.

What COVID-19 safety measures are in place?

We follow all Ministry of Health guidelines including temperature screening, hand sanitizing stations, social distancing in classrooms, regular disinfection of facilities, and health education for students.
