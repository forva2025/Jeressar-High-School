Why Choose Jeressar High School?

We provide a holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and innovation, preparing students for success in the global community.

Academic Excellence

Our competency-based curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and practical application of knowledge, ensuring students excel in national examinations.

Character Development

We instill strong moral values, leadership skills, and social responsibility through our comprehensive character education program and community service initiatives.

Innovation & Technology

State-of-the-art facilities, digital learning platforms, and STEM programs prepare students for the challenges and opportunities of the 21st century.

Our Academic Programs

Comprehensive curriculum designed to meet international standards while addressing local educational needs.

O-Level Program

Four-year lower secondary program following Uganda's competency-based curriculum with emphasis on practical skills and critical thinking.

  • 8-10 subjects including core sciences
  • Continuous assessment system
  • Digital learning integration
