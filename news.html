ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE September 15, 2024

Outstanding UCE Results 2024 - 98% Pass Rate

Jeressar High School has once again demonstrated its commitment to academic excellence with exceptional results in the 2024 Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations. Our students achieved an impressive 98% pass rate, with 15 students scoring in Division One.

The results reflect our dedicated teaching staff, modern facilities, and the hard work of our students. Notable performances include Sarah Auma, who scored 8 distinctions, and James Okello, who achieved 7 distinctions in science subjects.
