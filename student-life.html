What Our Community Says

Hear from our students, parents, and alumni about their experiences at Jeressar High School.

  • Sarah Auma

    Senior Five Student

    "Jeressar High School has been more than just an academic institution for me. The teachers here truly care about our success, and the various clubs have helped me discover my passion for science. I've grown not just academically, but as a confident young woman ready for university."

  • Mrs. Mary Okello

    Parent of S3 Student

    "Choosing Jeressar High School for my daughter was one of the best decisions we've made. The school's commitment to both academic excellence and character development is exceptional. The teachers are dedicated, and the facilities are top-notch. My daughter has blossomed into a confident, well-rounded individual."

  • Michael Otim

    Class of 2022, Currently at Makerere University

    "The foundation I received at Jeressar High School has been instrumental in my university success. The science club sparked my interest in engineering, and the dedicated teachers prepared me well for the rigors of university education. I'm grateful for the holistic education I received."

  • Mrs. Sarah Achieng

    Principal

    "What makes Jeressar High School special is our commitment to nurturing the whole child. We don't just focus on academic results; we cultivate character, creativity, and critical thinking. Seeing our students grow into confident, compassionate leaders is the greatest reward of my career."

  • Mr. David Ojok

    Local Community Leader

    "Jeressar High School has been a cornerstone of our community for years. The school's commitment to producing responsible citizens is evident in the graduates we see. They don't just excel academically; they come back to serve and uplift our community. This school is truly transforming lives and building our future."

  • Grace Atim

    Senior Four Student

    "Being a student at Jeressar High School has been an incredible journey. The teachers don't just teach; they mentor us and help us discover our potential. The drama club helped me overcome my shyness, and now I'm confident enough to lead student council meetings. This school really prepares you for life beyond the classroom."

  • Mr. James Akello

    Parent of S2 and S5 Students

    "Having two children at Jeressar High School, I can confidently say this institution goes above and beyond. The personalized attention each student receives is remarkable. My younger son struggled with mathematics, but the dedicated teachers provided extra support that helped him excel. Both my children have flourished here academically and socially."
