Our Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive educational programs following Uganda's national curriculum with modern teaching methodologies and state-of-the-art facilities.

O-Level Program

Lower Secondary (S1-S4)

Our four-year lower secondary program follows Uganda's competency-based curriculum, emphasizing practical skills, critical thinking, and holistic development.

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • History & Civics
  • Geography
  • Entrepreneurship

Elective Subjects

  • Agriculture
  • Art & Design
  • Computer Studies
  • Foreign Languages
  • Music
  • Physical Education
  • Technical Drawing
  • Home Economics

Assessment Method

Continuous Assessment (20%) + End of Cycle Examination (80%). Letter grades A-E with emphasis on practical application and critical thinking skills.
